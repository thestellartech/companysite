About AIRLENS

PerSapien Innovations is a Govt. of India recognized start-up established in 2017 by alumni from AIIMS, Stanford University & IIT. It is driven by the beliefs that basic healthcare should be available to all (Per) and that the right to life is fundamental for humankind (Sapien). Following the creed of “Health for All”, PerSapien passionately develops and facilitates technological and non-technological healthcare solutions that are user-friendly, accessible, and appreciated by all under the brand name of "Airlens".

We have been working for a problem that takes about 1.2 million lives each year in India i.e. Air Pollution, but with the onset of COVID, we pivoted ourselves to build new technologies and products to fight the pandemic.

Airlens N99 level Masks

Features and details of Airlens Masks are

  • N99 level filtration efficiency
  • World's 1st AMT(active molecular technology) - gives you the best protection from virus with comfortable breathability
  • Reusable & Washable up-to 20 times with lifespan of 2-3 months based on usage
  • It has a Bacteria Filtration capacity of 99.5+%
  • A powerful Particle Filtration Efficiency of 98.47% and filters as small as PM0.1 particles
  • HTD soft cotton fabric for best comfort and feel for long duration usage
  • Unique neck strap to avoid contamination from surfaces on to mask when not in use
  • 3 sizes(S,M & L) with ear adjuster to fit every face shape & size
  • Comes with a Sun Sanitizing HDPE zipper for each mask which helps in providing a longer mask life and quicker mask sanitization from natural sun rays
