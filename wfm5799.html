WorkForce Pro WF-M5799 Workgroup Monochrome Multifunction Printer with Replaceable Ink Pack System


Built For Your Business And The Environment.

Powered by PrecisionCore Heat-Free Technology, the WorkForce WF-M5799 delivers Performance Beyond Laser™ — all in a compact MFP that offers up to 60 percent lower printing costs vs. monochrome laser3. Featuring a 50-page duplex Auto Document Feeder and 40,000-page ink pack yield4, the WF-M5799 includes an innovative Replaceable Ink Pack System to minimize interventions. Designed for your busy workgroup, it prints pro-quality documents at 24 ISO ppm (black)†. Network setup and security features provide seamless integration and administration in managed IT environments. The WF-M5799 supports wired and wireless networking, plus PCL1 and PostScript® 3™.

  • MFP with WiFi® & Ethernet: Print | Copy | Scan | Fax | PCL1/PS
  • Ink: DURABrite® Ultra pigment ink
  • Print Speed: Up to 24 ppm†
