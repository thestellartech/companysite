Smart AV Solutions

Stellar, India is amongst the leading Smart AV solutions providers offering a range of solutions for Office Automation for Board Rooms, Conference rooms, Huddle room solutions, Auditoriums Network Operation Centre, Smart classrooms, Experience centre & Hospitality vertical. We have business relationship with all the leading AV principals named AMX, Atlona, Aurora, Aten, Crestron, Extron, Kramer, Lightware, Wyrestorm, Biamp, Harman, Bose, Bosch, QSC, Audio-Technica, Sennheiser, Beyerdynamic, Shure, Yamaha, Apart Audio, Music group, Revolabs, Sony, Lumens, Panasonic, Samsung, LG, Benq, Philips, Newline, Xtreme, Christie, Barco, Eyevis, Streme, Da-Lite, B-tech, etc. in order to provide cutting edge technology solutions.

We specialize in designing & building large and complex audio-visual projects. The designing is done in a futuristic way which enables the customer to adapt to changing technologies by making a minor modification in contrast to traditional rip-and-replace approach. We follow a very professional Project Management process to ensure that all our projects are completed in a timely fashion and meets the target deadlines of the customers accurately. We are associated with Infocomm and take up a host of technology trainings, in order to be abreast with the ever changing dynamics of the AV industry.

A strong sales & service presence across the span of the country ensures that we can cater to a multi-locational need of a customer and thereby provide a consistent user experience across the customer organization.
